Day 2 - 9 June 2026

The day began with a pleasant breakfast at BEL Guest House along with Brother Ranjith Kumar. The peaceful morning prepared us for an interesting day of exploration.

One of the highlights of the trip was the visit to the Indian Music Experience Museum. The interactive exhibits and rich musical history provided an engaging and educational experience for everyone.

Later we enjoyed lunch at the lively Church Street area before returning to the guest house. The day ended with a relaxed dinner and reflection on the wonderful experiences we had enjoyed.
