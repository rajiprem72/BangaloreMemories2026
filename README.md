# Bangalore Family Trip 2026 🚄❤️

A digital scrapbook preserving the memories of our wonderful 4-day family trip to Bangalore from **8 June 2026 to 11 June 2026**.

## About

This website was created to document and celebrate our family journey from Chennai to Bangalore aboard the Vande Bharat Express. During the trip we visited family and friends, explored shopping malls, enjoyed local attractions, experienced Bangalore's culture, and created many unforgettable memories together.

## Trip Timeline

### Day 1 – 8 June 2026

Departure from Chennai by Vande Bharat Express, lunch at Horamavu, visit to Phoenix Mall, family visits, and dinner at BEL Guest House.

### Day 2 – 9 June 2026

Indian Music Experience Museum, Church Street exploration, and quality family time.

### Day 3 – 10 June 2026

Orion Mall, Malleswaram, Commercial Street, and dinner with family at Padmanabapura Nagar.

### Day 4 – 11 June 2026

Visit to Lulu Mall and return journey to Chennai by Vande Bharat Express.
