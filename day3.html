Day 3 - 10 June 2026

After breakfast at BEL Guest House, we set out for another exciting day in Bangalore. Our first destination was Orion Mall, one of the city's most popular shopping and entertainment centers.

We later explored the charming neighborhood of Malleswaram, known for its traditional atmosphere and local culture. The combination of modern shopping and classic Bangalore heritage made the day particularly special.

Following lunch at Commercial Street, we spent the evening with family and friends. The day concluded with a wonderful dinner at Vinayak's house in Padmanabapura Nagar, creating memories that will stay with us for years.
